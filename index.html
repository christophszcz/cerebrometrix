Cerebrometrix

About

Brain Segmentation Simplified

efficient

Cerebrometrix provides fast, efficient, and reliable manual brain segmentations for Magnetic Resonance Images (MRI).

manual id

A key component of many MRI brain studies involve the manual identification and delineation of specific anatomical areas by an experienced anatomist. In this way, a variety of measures can be drawn from segmentations, including volume, morphology, and shape metrics.

cost-effective

Often a laborious and time-intensive process, Cerebrometrix aims to create a cost-effective solution for the manual segmentation required for any given research project.

experts

Our qualified team of anatomical experts can complete manual segmentation projects for a variety of MRI image types, brain structures, and populations, not limited to humans.

Robert S.C. Amaral

BSc. Hon, MSc. - Founder

Robert Amaral is the founder and director of Cerebrometrix. With over 5 years of neuroanatomical segmentation experience, Robert has been heavily involved in human neuroanatomical research in both healthy and clinical populations. This involvement has led him to achieve experience in the segmentation of numerous brain regions including both subcortical structures, and cortical areas.

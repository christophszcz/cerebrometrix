About

Brain Segmentation Simplified

Cerebrometrix provides fast, efficient, and reliable manual brain segmentations for Magnetic Resonance Images (MRI). A key component of many MRI brain studies involve the manual identification and delineation of specific anatomical areas by an experienced anatomist. In this way, a variety of measures can be drawn from segmentations, including volume, morphology, and shape metrics.

Often a laborious and time-intensive process, Cerebrometrix aims to create a cost-effective solution for the manual segmentation required for any given research project. Our qualified team of anatomical experts can complete manual segmentation projects for a variety of MRI image types, brain structures, and populations, not limited to humans.
